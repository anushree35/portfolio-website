About Me

Hey, I'm Anushree Sabade, a Computer Science student at the University of Connecticut. I love building innovative applications, solving complex problems, and leveraging technology to create meaningful impact.

My experience spans full-stack development, AI/ML, and data analysis. From developing AI-powered interview preparation tools to crafting dynamic web solutions, I'm always pushing the boundaries of what's possible with Python, JavaScript, Java, React, Flask, and cutting-edge AI technologies like OpenAI GPT-4.

My tech journey began with Girls Who Code, where I spent two transformative summers learning programming fundamentals and building my first projects. I've since expanded my skills through teaching coding to K-12 students and developing meaningful projects that solve real-world problems, including my latest AI Interview Preparation Tool that helps job seekers practice with personalized feedback.

I'm particularly fascinated by artificial intelligence and machine learning, exploring how technology can enhance human understanding and learning. My recent work includes integrating speech recognition, natural language processing, and real-time analytics to create intelligent applications. As a Girl Scout and active community volunteer, I'm committed to using my technical skills to serve others and make a meaningful impact.
